About me

I’m a passionate front-end web developer from Bihar, currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science at IES University. I love turning ideas into interactive and visually engaging digital experiences. Along with building modern, responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and C++, I also develop desktop applications. One of my key projects is EchoLock, a custom-built desktop application focused on smart voice-based system control, which reflects my interest in creating practical, real-world software solutions. I enjoy designing clean, user-friendly interfaces and bringing creativity into every project I work on. With over 5 self-driven projects completed, I’m constantly learning, experimenting with new tools, and pushing my limits as a developer. Currently, I’m exploring Python and backend development to grow into a full-stack developer. I genuinely enjoy solving problems through code and building products that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive, efficient, and impactful.
My goal is to create digital products—both web and desktop—that make life easier, feel smooth to use, and leave a strong impression on users.
